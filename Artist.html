About the Artist


El German

          Based in Baguio City, El German has dedicated most of her life creating dramatic and meaningful visuals. Her work focuses on the surrealism of the world, adeptly translating contemporary themes into a fresh realm of aesthetic allure and motivation. The 19-year-old artist harmonizes her unique expressions, creating a perfect blend of preposterous, whimsical, and an enduring spirit of discovery.

          She was born in Tarlac City and developed a keen interest in the arts at the tender age of five, initially focusing on sketching faces. What began as a mere pastime blossomed into one of her most cherished talents. At the age of 10, her grandmother observed her intricate drawings of architectural structures, a skill that demanded time and dedication to master. This recognition prompted her family to support her pursuit of a Bachelor of Science in Architecture. Today, she continues to follow this trajectory as a first-year student at Saint Louis University.

          Throughout her elementary and junior high school tenure, teachers frequently selected her to participate in a multitude of poster-making contests, spanning from internal school events to district-wide competitions. Although her involvement paused with the onset of the pandemic, El German persisted in pursuing her objectives. She earnestly delved into the realm of art, transitioning from graphite pencils to watercolors as her primary medium. Additionally, she ventured into digital artistry. Concurrently, her curiosity led her to explore political essay composition and prose, culminating in the creation of a personal anthology on the subject.

          At the age of 16, she embarked on undertaking projects for small businesses and collaborating with diverse writers. These ventures involved crafting book covers and business materials upon request. This initial opportunity served as a catalyst, propelling her towards a specialization in commissioned work, as her artistic prowess garnered increasing attention. Subsequently, clients entrusted her with tasks such as creating portraits, designing t-shirts, and other projects.
