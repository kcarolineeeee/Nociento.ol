Developer


Karla Caroline

          Karla Caroline Bisnan, known professionally as Caro, is an 18-year-old developer baseed in Baguio City, Philippines. Caro's journey began at a young age, driven by a fervent interest in crafting and enhancing websites encompassing both frontend and backend development. Presently, her endeavors predominantly center on frontend development, employing a blend of Bootstrap alongside JavaScript and CSS.

          Commencing her journey at the age of 11 while attending 7th grade, she was enrolled by her parents in a public school in Angeles City with a focus on Information and Communication Technology (ICT). Here, she not only acquired fundamental skills in website development but also honed her programming acumen. By the time Caro completed high school, she had proficiently mastered a multitude of programming languages, encompassing HTML, CSS, Turbo C, Visual Basic, and PHP (MySQL).

          This decision prompted Caro to pursue a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology at the University of Baguio. Currently in her first year of studies, she is excelling in the majority of her subjects, viewing this academic pursuit as a means to broaden her knowledge and equip herself for a successful future career in the industry.
